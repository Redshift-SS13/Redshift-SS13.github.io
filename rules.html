The Rules

These rules are intended to promote a safe and enjoyable roleplaying environment. If a player is breaking the rules, then adminhelp it via the F1 key or contact an admin directly after the round.


0. The spirit of the rules is more important than the letter.

  1. Don't be a dick.
  2. Play a believable character.
  3. Staffmembers aren't always right, but you still have to listen to them.

If you believe that a staffmember is abusing their power or is negligent in their duties, contact the Host or Administrator. Abusing your right to complain about staffmembers will result in administrative action.
